1. Overview

This report summarises field activities, community engagement outcomes, and resource utilisation for the second quarter of FY2026. Coverage spans 8 districts across 3 states, with active participation from 240 grassroots volunteers.

Quarter Highlight: Successful completion of 14 village-level participatory resource mapping exercises, covering a combined area of 18,400 hectares.

2. Key Activities

Community Engagement

  • Conducted 32 gram sabha sessions across Odisha, MP and Jharkhand
  • Enrolled 380 new community members into NRM self-help groups
  • Completed grievance redressal training for 60 village-level facilitators

Natural Resource Management

  • Demarcated 4 community forest resource (CFR) areas totalling 6,200 ha
  • Planted 12,000 saplings across 9 degraded patches
  • Installed 8 check-dams and 14 farm bunds under watershed programme
