Features

Everything your team needs

Built ground-up for ecological field organisations with complex document workflows across multiple regions.

Smart Document Upload
Upload PDFs, Word, Excel, PPTX and more. Tag documents by theme, category, and visibility with a streamlined form.
Approval Workflows
Built-in multi-step approval with Pending, Approved, Request to Change, and Rejected states tracked per document.
Role-Based Access
Visitor, Registered User, Org Moderator, and Org Administrator roles — each with tailored views and permissions.
Org-Level Reports
Visual reports covering user contributions, document themes, file type distribution, and activity summaries across time periods.
Advanced Search
Filter by theme, category, file type, visibility, uploader, and date range. Global search with instant document suggestions.
Secure & Controlled
Public and Private visibility controls per document. Owner-based edit/delete rights ensure your data stays protected.
Benefits

Why teams choose FES DMS

Designed with field teams in mind — simple, fast, and reliable across regions.

Fast & Lightweight
Optimised for low-bandwidth environments. Field teams can access and upload documents without high-speed internet.
Secure by Default
Role-gated access ensures sensitive documents are only visible to authorised users within the organisation.
Organised by Theme
Categorise documents by NRM, Forest, Livelihood, Water, Land Rights and 8 more themes for instant discoverability.
Actionable Insights
Track uploads, downloads, active users, and theme coverage with a live reports dashboard updated in real time.
Multi-Organisation Support
Manage multiple organisations and districts from a single platform with dedicated admin controls per organisation.
Full Audit Trail
Every upload, approval, and status change is logged with timestamps, giving complete visibility into document history.
Who It's For

Built for every stakeholder

Four distinct roles, each with a tailored experience and the right level of access.

VS
Visitor
Browse public documents and search the catalogue without an account commitment.
RU
Registered User
Upload, manage, and track personal documents through the full approval workflow.
OM
Org Moderator
Review and approve submissions, access org-level reports, and manage document quality.
OA
Org Administrator
Full control over users, roles, organisations, and complete reporting visibility.
