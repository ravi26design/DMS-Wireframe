About FES DMS

Helping field teams manage ecological knowledge

FES DMS is a purpose-built Document Management System for the Foundation for Ecological Security — bringing structure, security, and visibility to documents that drive ecological restoration across India.

Our Mission

To give field organisations a single, reliable home for their documents — so that knowledge gathered across districts and themes is never lost, always discoverable, and securely shared with the right people.

Our Vision

A future where every ecological field team — however remote — can collaborate on documents as easily as the largest organisation, with full transparency from upload to approval.

Our Story

Built for the field, from the field

The Foundation for Ecological Security (FES) works across thousands of villages on the restoration of land, water, and forests. With teams spread across districts and themes — from Natural Resource Management to Land & Tenure Rights — documents were scattered across drives, inboxes, and devices.

FES DMS was created to change that. Instead of hunting for the latest version of a survey or a report, teams now have one organised, searchable platform with built-in approval workflows and role-based access — designed to work even in low-bandwidth environments.

Today it manages thousands of documents across multiple organisations and districts, giving moderators and administrators a clear, real-time view of everything happening on the ground.

What We Value

Principles that guide us

Every feature in FES DMS is shaped by the realities of field work.

Security First

Sensitive field data stays protected with role-based access and public/private visibility controls on every document.

Simplicity

Lightweight and fast, built to work for field teams on low-bandwidth connections without a steep learning curve.

Transparency

A full audit trail means every upload, approval, and status change is logged and visible to the right people.
